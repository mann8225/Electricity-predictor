About This Project

The Bhopal Electricity Predictor is an AI-powered tool designed to help residents and businesses in Bhopal forecast their monthly electricity consumption. By leveraging historical data, seasonal trends, and machine learning models, our system generates reliable predictions that can aid in budgeting and energy conservation planning.

üîç How It Works

Users simply select a year and month. The system feeds these inputs to a trained regression model, which then estimates the total electricity usage for that period. The model accounts for patterns such as increased consumption in summer and decreased usage in winter. Results are visualized through charts and can also be downloaded as reports.
